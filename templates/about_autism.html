What is Autism?

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a developmental condition that affects how a person communicates, interacts, and experiences the world. It is called a "spectrum" because it includes a wide range of strengths, challenges, and needs.

  • Common signs: Difficulty with social interaction, repetitive behaviors, unique strengths and differences.
  • Symptoms: May include challenges in communication, sensitivity to sensory input, and preference for routines.
  • Support: Early intervention, understanding, and acceptance can help people with autism thrive.

Remember: Autism is not an illness. Many people with autism have unique talents and perspectives that enrich our communities. Acceptance and support make a big difference!
