About Us

Autism Predictor is a project dedicated to raising awareness and supporting early detection of Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) using modern technology.

Our mission is to make autism screening more accessible and to empower families, educators, and healthcare professionals with easy-to-use tools. We believe that early awareness and understanding can make a positive impact in the lives of people with autism and their loved ones.

This website uses machine learning to provide a quick, informative prediction based on user input. We encourage everyone to use this tool as a first step and to seek professional advice for any concerns.

Together, we can build a more inclusive and supportive world for all.
